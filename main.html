Div Shooter

2013

This game is made with solely html divs. Hundreds of divs. Moreover, it looks like they are in 3D. It is a shooting game where player claps his hand to shoot and move his head to avoid crashing into the divs. In order to augment the 3D effect, the angle from which the 3D content is shown will change according to the eye positions of a player so that he/she will actually "feel" the 3D more realistically.
